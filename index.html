About Depot Miki

🍽️

Serving Balikpapan
with Love & Flavor

⏰
Open Daily 06:00 – 18:00

Depot Miki is a popular breakfast restaurant in Balikpapan known for authentic Indonesian dishes.

We serve traditional meals such as nasi kuning, soto banjar, bubur ayam, and bakmi ayam — crafted with fresh ingredients and generations of culinary tradition.

Our goal is to provide delicious food at affordable prices in a comfortable, welcoming environment.
